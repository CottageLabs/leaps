Thanks for submitting your form

Your input is greatly appreciated and will make your interview with LEAPS staff quicker and easier. To find out more about what your LEAPS interview will involve, have a look at the LEAPS S6 - Blog.

LEAPS want to explore the effectiveness of the LEAPS Pre-UCAS 1:1 Interview, and we ask for your help to do this! We invite you to complete this short online questionnaire to help us gather a sense of how LEAPS-eligible pupils feel about applying to university before their LEAPS Pre-UCAS Interview.

The questionnaire should take no more than 5 minutes to complete. Completing the questionnaire allows us to make sure LEAPS support is as effective as possible for future LEAPS pupils. You can complete the questionnaire here:

https://edinburgh.onlinesurveys.ac.uk/pre-leaps-interview-questionnaire

If you are completing this form using a PC in a public place, you should now take the recommended precautions to ensure nobody else can access your session and the information you submitted. For example, close the internet browser and log off the PC.
